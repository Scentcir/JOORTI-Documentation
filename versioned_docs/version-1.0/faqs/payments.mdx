# Payments

<details open>
  <summary>Is it possible to have double discounts on my order?</summary>
  <div>
    <div>
      Unfortunately we do not accept multiple discounts. One coupon per purchase only. Also, discounts cannot be applied to already-discounted items.
    </div>
    <br />
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What payment methods do you accept?</summary>
  <div>
    <div>
      Right now we only accept PayPal payments.
    </div>
    <br />
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Do you accept insurance?</summary>
  <div>
    <div>
      Unfortunately, we are not currently affiliated with any insurance companies. However, we can provide you with a detailed copy of your invoice so you may request reimbursement from your insurance company. Check with your insurance company, and if they do, you can request an emailed invoice copy from our Customer Service department or you can download it in your account.
    </div>
    <br />
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Can I get an invoice?</summary>
  <div>
    <div>
     Invoice is not included in our parcels. You can contact our customer service support (support#ineglasses.com).
    </div>
    <br />
  </div>
</details>
